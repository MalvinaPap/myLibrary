Bulk Update Books

üìã Update Instructions
  • Each upload should contain 2 columns only: bookId (the unique ID of the book to update) and one other column to update. Accepted columns are: title, author, genre, and notes.

    Example:
    bookId,title
    1,New Title for Book 1
    2,New Title for Book 2

    Important: Only the fields included in the CSV will be updated. Other fields will remain unchanged.
    Make sure the bookId values correspond to existing books in your library.
    You can find the bookId by exporting your library data as CSV.
